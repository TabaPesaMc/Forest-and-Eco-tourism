ABOUT US

Usambara Forestry and Eco Tourism Forum is a non Governmental Organization that is Operating it's activities in Lushoto,TangaTanzania.The Organization Mainly Focuses on the Primary activities.Planting Trees Around Communities,Enhancing Eco Tourism i.e Green Tourism.

Usambara Forestry and Eco Tourism Forum has been doing it's activities on planting variety Trees Especially in Schools and Institutions as well as Providing General Environmental Education Our Society.We hope to Expand more Country Widely to Facilitate Environmental Protection and Corporate with Other Environmental Stakeholders to Ensure The Green Earth And to Comply With Millennium Goals.

.Through planting trees we hope to involve highly our communities which are the highly beneficianes of the Projects to be carried out by the Organization. Farmore we hope to facilitate on the Green tourism ,as we hope to facilitate the preservation of old age Customs which have been used by our Fore fathers through initiative ind mutual sharing the societal customs. Climate Action also has been our major concern we hope to plant as much trees as possible to reduce the Green house gases impacts and also to ensure stable season of rainfalls which will help our communities to conduct agricultural activities well.Also Through planting fruits trees we hope to expand our community income.

Our Mission & Vision

Vision

A clean healthy And well Protected Environmental Supporting Though Planting Trees And Eco Tourism to Enhance Sustainable Society And Economy of Usamabara Mountains Western Arc Communities.

Mission

To Protect and Improve The Environments Including Forests as Valuable Asset for thecommunities Around Usamabara Forests.And to Protect our People and Forests from Pollution while Supporting Green Tourism .
